Plants with Pants

Plants with Pants is committed to establishing lasting connections with developing communities to empower self-sufficiency and provide low cost, high quality food alternaties. We consider ourselves a community of our own composed not only of talented farmers and knowledgeble teachers but also of everyday citizens each with their own talent. Through a variety of outreach methods Plants with Pants has had a hand in creating over 300+ community gardens and urban growing centers across the United States.
