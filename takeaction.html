What You Can Do To Help

Group Planting Events

Come and have a good time planting with us. At Plants With Pants we treat everyone as a family and work as a group to plant as much as we can.

Sustainable Ways of Living

Not only do we try to keep our environament healthy by planting plants, but we also try our best to practice sustainable ways of living. We want everyone to live a sustainable life for the better.

A couple things you could do to live a more sustainable life are

  • Trying to recycle
  • Finding more eco friendly ways of transportation
  • Solar energy
  • And many other ways

If WE ALL come together to live and try out best then we will have a better future for ourselves.

Spreading The Word

Try to tell as much people as you can. Spread the word. We can only do so much as an organization but with your help more people will take the initiative to make the world a better place for the environment
